FAQ

Do you compress my photos?

No—originals stay original. You can optionally create lighter copies for sharing.

What does end-to-end encryption mean?

Files are encrypted on your device before upload; only paired devices can decrypt.

Can I auto-delete after sync?

Yes. Auto-Clean removes local copies after verified sync. Restore depends on your plan.

Do I need an account?

Not for quick one-off sends. For history, restore, and multi-device libraries, sign up.
